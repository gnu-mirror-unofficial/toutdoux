Application
The 'Gantt' plug-in in action.
All applications can be integrated by plug-in.
In fact, the document (XML) contains structure, plug-in parameters and datatables.
When the document is opened, the necessary plug-ins are loaded.

Construction
The database's structure is user-defined.
